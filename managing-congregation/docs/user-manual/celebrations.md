## ðŸ’Œ Card Generator

Create custom, high-quality birthday cards in seconds without design skills.

### 1. Select a Design Style

Use the **Font Selection** dropdown at the top of the page to change the aesthetic of the cards immediately.

-   **Caveat**: Playful, handwriting style. Good for informal greetings.
-   **Fleur De Leah**: Elegant, script font. Best for formal anniversaries.
-   **Roboto**: Clean, modern sans-serif.

![Font Selection Dropdown - Screenshot](../images/celebrations-fonts.png)

### 2. Preview the Card

Click the **Preview** button next to a member's name. A modal will pop up showing exactly what the card looks like.

**The card automatically includes:**

-   Member's Name
-   "Happy Birthday" (or Anniversary) message
-   Community Name (in the footer)
-   Festive graphics (cake, confetti, cross)

![Card Preview Modal - Screenshot](../images/card-preview.png)

### 3. Share

You have two options:

-   **Download**: Saves the card as a high-resolution PNG image to your computer. You can then print it or send it via WhatsApp/Telegram.
-   **Email**: Instantly sends the card to the member's registered email address. You will see a success message once sent.

![Email Success Notification - Screenshot](../images/email-success-toast.png)
