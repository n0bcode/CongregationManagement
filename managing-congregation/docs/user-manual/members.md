# Member Management

The **Members** module is the core of the system, allowing for detailed tracking of every individual in the congregation.

![Members List View - Screenshot](../images/members-list.png)
_Figure 1: The main members list with search and filter options._

## üë§ Member Profiles

Each member has a comprehensive profile containing personal data, formation history, and documents.

### Creating a New Member

1.  Navigate to **Members** in the sidebar.
2.  Click the **Add New** button in the top right.
3.  **Fill in the details**:
    -   **Name** (Required): Legal or religious name.
    -   **Religious Name**: If applicable.
    -   **Date of Birth**: Essential for age tracking.
    -   **Status**: Select "Active", "Deceased", or "Exited".
4.  Click **Save**.

![Create New Member Form - Screenshot](../images/member-create-form.png)

### Editing a Member

Click on any member's name in the list to view their full profile. Use the **Edit** button to update details.

The system tracks the member's journey through religious life.

![Formation Timeline - Screenshot](../images/member-formation-timeline.png)
_Figure 2: Visual timeline of a member's vow and ordination history._

### Adding a Formation Event

1.  Go to the member's profile.
2.  Scroll to the **Formation** section.
3.  Click **Add Event**.
4.  Select the **Stage** (e.g., First Vows, Final Vows).
5.  Enter the **Date**.
6.  (Optional) Upload a scanned copy of the certificate.

## üìÇ Documents & Health

Securely store private information.

### Document Storage

Upload scans of Passports, ID cards, or Academic Degrees.

1.  Click the **Documents** tab.
2.  Drop files into the upload area or click **Browse**.
3.  You can categorize documents (e.g., "Legal", "Education").

![Document Upload Section - Screenshot](../images/member-documents.png)

### Health Records

Track medical history, allergies, or insurance info.

-   **Add Record**: Click **New Health Record**, enter the date and details.
-   **Privacy**: These records are only visible to authorized roles (e.g., Super Admin, Medical Liaison).

![Health Records Tab - Screenshot](../images/member-health.png)

### Assigning to a New Community

1.  Go to the **Assignments** tab in the member's profile.
2.  Click **New Assignment**.
3.  Select the **Target Community** from the dropdown.
4.  Select the **Start Date**.
    -   _Note_: The system will automatically set the "End Date" of their previous assignment to the day before this new one starts.
5.  Click **Save**.
