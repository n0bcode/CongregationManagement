# Community Management

The **Communities** module helps you manage the physical houses, residence halls, or mission stations and the members assigned to them.

![Communities List View - Screenshot](../images/communities-list.png)
_Figure 1: List of all active communities._

## ðŸ˜ Managing Communities

A "Community" represents a physical location or organizational unit.

### Community Details

Each community record tracks:

-   **Name**: The official name.
-   **Patron Saint**: The spiritual protector of the house.
-   **Feast Day**: The date of celebration.
-   **Foundation Date**: When it was established.
-   **Contact**: Address, Phone, and Email.

### Creating a Community

1.  Go to **Communities**.
2.  Click **Create Community**.
3.  Fill in the form.
    -   _Tip_: Adding a correct "Feast Day" will trigger reminders on the Dashboard.
4.  Click **Save**.

Switch to the **History** tab to see a log of everyone who has _ever_ lived in this community, including their start and end dates.

## ðŸ“Š Annual Reports

Generate specialized reports for inspections or year-end reviews.

1.  Navigate to **Reports > Community Annual**.
2.  Select the **Community**.
3.  Select the **Year**.
4.  Click **Generate**.

![Community Annual Report - Screenshot](../images/community-report-preview.png)

The report includes:

-   List of members present at the start of the year.
-   Arrivals (Transfers In).
-   Departures (Transfers Out).
-   Deaths or Exits.
